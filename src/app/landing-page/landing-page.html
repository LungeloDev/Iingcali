About Us

IINGCALI Media & Digital Consultancy is a 100% black female–owned company founded and directed by Siwaphiwe Myataza, a communications professional, PR strategist and brand manager with extensive experience in radio, digital marketing, broadcasting and public affairs.

We specialise in content creation and management that aligns with your brand values, producing commercial scripts, thought-leadership material, video productions, podcasts and social campaigns that resonate.

Beyond content, we partner with organisations on digital transformation and brand identity development, guiding teams through strategy, crisis communications and market research.

Our goal is simple: to empower brands, corporates, NGOs and media partners with meaningful stories and sustainable strategies — helping them connect authentically with audiences while achieving real impact.
